# Hero Image Instructions

Please upload your hero image file to this folder and name it `hero.png`

You can do this through GitHub:
1. Go to https://github.com/Rdiep78/Projects/tree/claude/setup-gtm-strategy-app-01CDUUJXz2TgKWoYh1hUwWDA/images
2. Click "Add file" > "Upload files"
3. Upload your image as `hero.png`

Once uploaded, the image will automatically appear on your site.
